Overview

Our project, which secured the first prize at the Zhejiang University Industrial Design Competition, presents a novel approach to pet memorialization. The concept, Eternal Bloom, transforms the traditional method of commemorating beloved pets by integrating their memory with the growth of nature.

Eternal Bloom is an innovative product that allows pet owners to place their pet's remains in a biodegradable container designed to nurture new life. As time progresses, grass grows from the container, symbolizing the continuation of the pet's life and providing a tangible, living reminder of the cherished companion. This design not only offers a new solution for pet ashes but also creates a meaningful bond between the owner and their pet, even after they have passed away.
