Overview

In the course of User Experience and Product Innovation Design (Prof. Shijian Luo), we secured a collaboration with HP Singapore to enhance HP’s LinkedIn page interface.

Through comprehensive research methods including on-site visits to HP Singapore, user interviews, competitor analysis, and data-driven design strategies, we thoroughly analyzed the existing LinkedIn page. Our team identified key areas for improvement and iteratively developed the final UI design. This process involved creating low-fidelity and high-fidelity prototypes, conducting usability testing, and refining the design based on user feedback. The optimized interface aims to improve user engagement, streamline the job search process, and better showcase HP’s brand culture and job opportunities.

Data Analysis

We conducted data analysis to uncover user behavior patterns and preferences, which informed our design decisions. We also created visualizations to present key metrics and ad performance, enhancing stakeholder understanding and facilitating data-driven design improvements.

User Test

We conducted a mini user test to evaluate our UI optimization. The results showed significant improvements in task completion rates, with an increase of about 5%. Users found the design intuitive, with clear navigation and visually appealing elements that enhanced their overall experience. Feedback highlighted the ease of finding important functions on the first use and the professional design of icons that made their functions immediately clear.
