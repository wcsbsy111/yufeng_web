Service Design of a Justice Online Themed Restaurant

← Back to Selected Works

Overview

This project was developed as part of a collaborative service design course jointly offered by Zhejiang University and NetEase. Our team stood out with excellent performance throughout the project and was ultimately awarded internship offers from NetEase as recognition of our design capability.

The project explores the creation of a Justice Online (逆水寒) themed restaurant, aiming to integrate immersive game elements into a real-world dining experience. Through in-depth user research, field studies, and service innovation strategies, we designed a space that merges storytelling, interactivity, and social engagement. The concept not only extends the influence of the Justice Online IP beyond the digital realm but also creates a new cultural and commercial ecosystem where fans, food lovers, and casual visitors can all enjoy a uniquely immersive experience.

Restaurant Brand Research

Brand Research Summary

  1. Ordering UI Design: The ordering UI is beautifully designed, showcasing a fresh and simple style.
  2. Artistic Dish Naming: The dish categories are cleverly and artistically named, such as "Asking About Tea, Knowing the Eight Immortals" and "Tea-Crusted Pastries with Yummy Tea." These unique names make ordering more engaging for customers.
  3. Main Course Pricing and Variety: Meat dishes like potato beef brisket and Longjing roasted chicken generally cost 50-60 yuan per portion, while vegetarian dishes like Xishi tofu are around 30 yuan per portion. This pricing is within the generally acceptable price range.
  4. Extensive Snacks: A wide variety of snacks are available, priced around 20 yuan per portion. These snacks are typically small but exquisite, allowing customers to enjoy a variety of delicious snacks while savoring their main courses, enhancing their dining experience.
  5. Specialty Drinks and Snacks: Customers are offered a variety of unique drinks and snacks, enriching the dining experience.
  6. Optimized space layout: The restaurant's space layout has been carefully optimized and divided into different themed areas, creating a comfortable and private dining environment for customers and enhancing the overall dining experience.

Promotional Design

The project is scheduled to open during the May Day holiday, capitalizing on peak tourism and consumption periods and expected to attract a large number of tourists. Data shows that domestic tourists reached 270 million during the 2023 May Day holiday, a 70.8% year-on-year increase, demonstrating a strong desire to travel and spend. A successful opening will lay a solid foundation for subsequent summer marketing activities and help build customer reputation and loyal customers. The promotional outline is designed as follows:

Promotional Outline

Online Promotion: Engage and promote the brand through player-submitted figurine designs, cosplay, fan art competitions, reposting prize draws, and online advertising, as well as visual materials and event information on official platforms.

Offline Promotion: Host restaurant opening ceremonies with prominent guests, place offline advertisements, display posters, establish targeted promotional locations, increase on-site interaction and media coverage, and enhance brand exposure.

In-store Promotion: Enhance the customer experience and interaction through photo check-in spots, live streams, game highlights, storytelling sessions, merchandise displays and exchanges, and NPC interactive encounters.

Post-customer experience: Collect customer feedback, organize check-in photos, encourage social media sharing, gather suggestions for improvements, and amplify promotional efforts through customer interaction and sharing.

Feasibility analysis

01 Market Analysis

Target Market: Players and fans of Against the Water are the primary target group. These individuals are passionate about and loyal to the game and are willing to pay for related offline experiences. Store-goers and art enthusiasts are secondary target groups. Against the Water's more distinctive fashions and other items can attract these types of customers to check out.

Market Demand: There are currently some theme restaurants in the market, but restaurants featuring high-quality IPs like Against the Water are relatively scarce, and market demand is high.

02 Financial Analysis

Startup Costs: Initial investment includes venue rental, renovation, equipment purchases, staff training, and initial operating capital. A detailed budget is required to ensure sufficient start-up capital and avoid capital constraints.

Revenue Forecast: Analyze the revenue data of similar theme restaurants and combine it with the fan base of Ni Shui Han to create a preliminary revenue forecast. Consider customer spending habits and spending power to establish a reasonable pricing strategy.

Cost Control: Control raw material procurement costs, employee wages, and operating costs through refined management. Leverage supply chain management and long-term partnerships to reduce procurement costs.

03 Operational Analysis

Service and Management: High-quality service and management are key to success. Regularly assess employee authenticity to maintain the stability of the restaurant's ecosystem. Establish rational service processes and management mechanisms to ensure operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Marketing Strategy: Utilize a hybrid online and offline marketing strategy, promoting through social media, gaming communities, and fan groups. Regularly organize themed events, interactive experiences, and fan meet-and-greets to enhance customer loyalty and retention.
