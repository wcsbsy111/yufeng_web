VR+EMS root canal surgery simulation teaching system

← Back to Selected Works

Overview

The global dental services market's robust growth underscores a critical need for sophisticated training solutions. Our VR-based simulation addresses the scarcity and expense of practical training resources and the variability in training quality due to reliance on instructor experience.

Drawing on interviews with dental students and professionals, we pinpointed key training challenges: locating root canals, cleaning techniques, and precise filling methods. Our solution offers a high-fidelity simulation that mirrors the complexity of real procedures, enhancing precision and tactile feedback. The system's VR component provides real-time visual guidance, while EMS gloves offer realistic tactile feedback, and AI-driven scoring delivers objective performance assessments. This triad of technologies ensures a comprehensive, engaging, and effective learning platform.
